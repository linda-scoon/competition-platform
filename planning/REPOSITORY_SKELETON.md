## Required file naming rules

Use these exact names. Do not rename them.

## Required contents

- Files in `docs/` are the approved source-of-truth product documents.
- Files in `planning/` are the AI operating system for implementation.
- Files in `.github/` provide a reusable structure even if PRs are not used yet.
- Files in `TO_BE_ADDED_BY_HUMAN/` must be filled by the human owner.
